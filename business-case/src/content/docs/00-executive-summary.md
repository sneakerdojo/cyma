## What Octio is

**Octio is a pure-play AI company in Pretoria, South Africa, that builds, deploys, and operates autonomous AI agents for small and mid-sized service businesses.** We don't build websites. We don't run ad campaigns. We deploy the AI agents that take a business's existing traffic and turn it into qualified phone calls, booked discovery calls, sent newsletters, and posted social media — without the business hiring anyone.

We are our own Patient Zero: every product Octio sells runs Octio's own operations first. The AI lead-generation chat on `octio.co.za` is the *same* AI Lead Generation product we sell to customers, just configured for our tenant first.

## The market

- 2.5M+ SMMEs in South Africa
- SMMEs contribute ~34% of GDP and ~60% of employment ([SAP Africa](https://news.sap.com/africa/2026/03/the-essential-tech-trends-for-african-smes/))
- 76% of small businesses globally are actively using or exploring AI in 2026, up from 40% a year prior ([SBE Council](https://sbecouncil.org/2026/04/25/the-ai-tools-small-businesses-are-using/))
- South African AI market projected to reach billions in spend with marketing + customer engagement leading SMB adoption ([Statista](https://www.statista.com/outlook/tmo/artificial-intelligence/south-africa))
- The local-services niche we target (plumbers, electricians, HVAC, dentists, attorneys, contractors, accountants, salons) is ~250,000 businesses in SA alone

**Why now:** in 2026 SMBs have stopped experimenting with AI and started buying it — but only when ROI is measurable. Marketing + customer engagement leads adoption because the ROI is the most legible (more calls, more bookings, more revenue).

## How we win

Three structural advantages over the field:

1. **Pure-play AI, not "AI as a feature".** Our competitors are website agencies / marketing firms who added AI as a side offering. Octio is AI-first. The website you have is your business; the AI agents we deploy are the staff you didn't have to hire.
2. **Patient Zero credibility.** Every product Octio sells, Octio runs. When a prospect asks "does this work?", we point to our own lead funnel — the chat they're literally talking to is the product.
3. **Recursive selling.** We use our products to sell our products: the website audit captures leads, the chat qualifies them, the voice agent picks up follow-ups, the newsletter nurtures the long-tail, the social media manager fills the top of funnel. Customer acquisition cost compounds downward as the product portfolio matures.

## The bet

Bootstrapped, founder-led, 12-month plan:

| Window | Milestone | Customer count target |
|---|---|---|
| Days 1–7 | Every product MVP shipped + first paying customer | 1–3 |
| Days 8–30 | First cohort (founder hand-delivered, Wizard-of-Oz wherever needed) | 5–10 |
| Months 2–3 | Self-serve onboarding + audit funnel doing measurable acquisition | 15–30 |
| Months 4–6 | Profitable on services + recurring; refine product quality from real usage | 40–80 |
| Months 7–12 | Scale via affiliates + content + paid + agency partnerships | 150–300 |

At 200 customers × average R6,500/month, we hit R15.6M ARR. That's the 12-month north star. Achievable as a bootstrapped solo-founder operation only because the products do the work — automation is the leverage, not headcount.
