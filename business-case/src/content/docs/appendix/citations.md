## Methodology note

We've cited as much as we can. Where claims are unsourced (e.g., "30–60% of inbound leads lost to slow response in service businesses"), they reflect founder operational experience or industry conventional wisdom. Material decisions in the business case (TAM, pricing, growth rates) are anchored to publicly cited sources or internal data.

If you spot a missing or weak citation, please raise it — every claim should be defensible.
